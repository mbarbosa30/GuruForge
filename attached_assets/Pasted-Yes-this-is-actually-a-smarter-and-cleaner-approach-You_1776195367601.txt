How the system will operate (automatic & invisible to the creator)

Conversation Step (what the user experiences) → Always uses the full flagship model the creator chose (Grok 4.20 or GPT-5.4). This is where personality, depth, and “wisdom” matter most.
Triage Step (quick analysis of every incoming message) → Always uses the fast/cheap variant (Grok 4.1 Fast or GPT-5 Mini). Keeps latency under 300–400 ms.
Calibration Step (learning & contribution scoring after every reply) → Also uses the fast/cheap variant. This is where most of the cost and background work happens, so we keep it efficient.

This routing is fully automatic and transparent in the creator dashboard:
“Your Guru is powered by Grok 4.20 for conversations and Grok 4.1 Fast for background learning (saving ~60–70% on costs).”
Benefits of this 2-option approach

Extremely simple for creators → higher completion rate on the publish form.
We still deliver near-top-tier intelligence.
Cost control is excellent (creators only pay for the heavy model when the user is actually chatting).
Easy to expand later (add Claude, Gemini, or “Custom” as a third/fourth option).
